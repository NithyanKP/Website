Optical Signal Processing

Optical Signal Processing is an interdisciplinary topic that brings together various fields of Optics and digital signal processing. The techniques relies mostly on exploiting various nonlinear physical phenomena and devices to achieve high bandwidth, advanced modulation format and high speed processing of optical signal without any optical or electo-optical conversion.
